Karol G

Karol G is a Colombian singer who is breaking records and making her name known in the music industry. At only 30-years-old, the singer is making history on various charts and platforms such as Billboard's Top Latin Charts and Spotify.

Karol G Wins Album Of The Year At Latin Grammys!

Karol G won the Latin Grammy for Album Of The Year for Mañana Será Bonito at thw 2023 Latin Grammys.

The Colombian artist Karol G launched her fourth studio album, "Mañana Será Bonito," on February 24, 2023. The album was distributed by Universal Music Latino.

"Mañana Será Bonito" is made up of seventeen songs, mainly in the reggaeton genre. It includes collaborations with artists such as Romeo Santos, Quevedo, Shakira, Justin Quiles, Ángel Dior, Maldy, Bad Gyal, Sean Paul, Sech, Ovy on the Drums, and Carla Morrison.
